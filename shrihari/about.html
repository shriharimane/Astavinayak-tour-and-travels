About Ashtavinayak Tours

Your Sacred Journey to Eight Divine Temples

Welcome to Ashtavinayak Tour & Travels

Ashtavinayak Tour & Travels is dedicated to providing unforgettable pilgrimage experiences to the eight sacred Ganpati temples across Maharashtra. Established in 2015, we have been serving devotees and pilgrims with utmost care and spiritual dedication.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make the sacred Ashtavinayak pilgrimage accessible to everyone. We believe in creating meaningful spiritual journeys that bring peace, blessings, and divine connection to our travelers. Each temple visit is carefully planned to ensure maximum comfort and spiritual fulfillment.

The Eight Sacred Temples

The Ashtavinayak temples represent eight different manifestations of Lord Ganesha, each with its own unique spiritual significance:

1. Morgaon - Maha Ganapati | 2. Siddhatek - Siddha Ganapati | 3. Pali - Ballal Ganapati | 4. Ranjangaon - Ranjana Ganapati | 5. Theur - Chintamani Ganapati | 6. Lenadhya - Girijatmaja Ganapati | 7. Mandhardevi - Vighnahartha Ganapati | 8. Ozar - Vikat Ganapati
